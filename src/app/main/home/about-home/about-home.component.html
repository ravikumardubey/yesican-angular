YES I CAN - Academy

Transformation Academy "Sarathi": This academy aims to transform and coach the professionals and young generations to be successful & happy, which are significant Indicators of the battlefield (Kurikshetra) of life. Success and Happiness are subjective and can vary from person to person.

It's essential to have a coaching and transformation program for each individual's unique needs and goals while keeping these overarching principles in mind, called MESH (Make Everyone Successful and Happy) and act like "SARATHI," which was done by Lord Krishna to Arjuna in the ballet of Mahabharata. The transformation is based on a "Change in the process of thinking," leading to an inner feeling of "Yes, I Can" by germinating seeds of Positive thoughts by training the brain.
